About Me

Building a party that fights for working people and uplifts youth voices

I'm a democratic organizer committed to electing bold progressives and building grassroots youth movements that fight for everyday people and reject special interests.

As a young person in politics, I am used to being sidelined and feeling like I don't belong, but I will continue to show up, as we all must, to elect people that fight for us.

From local democratic central committees to congressional campaigns, people my age may be the next generation of voters, but we are this generation of organizers.

My three pillars of leadership:
  • Organizing: Supporting others, having real conversations with everyday people, building people-oriented campaigns.
  • Representation: Uplifting youth and marginalized voices in a world where far too many go unheard.
  • Accountability: Calling out our leaders when they sell us out for personal or special interests.
